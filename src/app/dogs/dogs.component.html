CALLIE

Female

3 years old

weight: 25 lbs

Callie is very athletic and a master of the frisbee arts. She is very intelligent and eager to please. Her day-to-day includes trips to the dog park and splashing in the creek. She's a one person dog and it takes a while to warm up to strangers, but once she has, she becomes extremely affectionate. Sometimes (I'll be honest), her love is overwhelming!

SMOKEY

Male

1 year old

Smokey's favorite passtime is running. Running after pesky chipmunks, running for tennis balls, or running after his best friend Callie. He is the friendliest dog, known for cuddling with strangers and falling asleep in their arms. He requires a lot of exercise but don't worry about setting your alarm for ball at the crack of dawn, he is sure to lick you until you get up!
